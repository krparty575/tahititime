Tahiti Time, Est. 2022


Tahiti Time is a newly built beach house located near the idyllic Tahiti Beach in Elbow Cay, Abaco, Bahamas. It is designed for up to 20 guests and features 7 bedrooms and 6 and a half bathrooms - it's perfect for large families or groups. Enjoy beautiful sunsets from the wraparound porch, with views of the Sea of Abaco and Atlantic Ocean.

Rental and Food Recommendations

Golf Cart Rental Recommendations

You can easily get around the island via golf cart, which can be rented from companies such as Getaway Cart Rentals and Hope Town Cart Rentals. There's golf cart parking underneath the house, in town, and at every restaurant and business around the island.

Boat Rental Recommendations

There's a boat slip included with the house rental (located at Dorros Cove dock). For boat rentals, we recommend Island Marine and Barefoot Boat Rentals.
Need to hire a boat captain? Let us know and we'll be happy to put you in touch with a local captain.

Food Shopping & Dining

There are 3 grocery stores on Elbow Cay - LVA, Vernon's Grocery, and Harbour View. But the best place to shop is Maxwell's in Marsh Harbour (it has the largest variety). Also, make sure you check out these restaurants: On Da Beach, Abaco Inn , Firefly, Mackey's , Willie's, Hope Town Inn and Marina, and Cap'n Jack's.
