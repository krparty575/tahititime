Tahiti Time, Est. 2022


Tahiti Time is a newly built beach house located near the idyllic Tahiti Beach in Elbow Cay, Abaco, Bahamas. It is designed for up to 20 guests. The main house features 7 bedrooms and 6 and a half bathrooms, sleeping 18. There is also an optional Captain’s Quarters that can be added on to your rental, sleeping 2 additional guests (with bedroom, bathroom, and kitchenette). It’s perfect for large families or groups. Enjoy beautiful sunsets from the wraparound porch, with views of the Sea of Abaco and Atlantic Ocean.

Rental and Food Recommendations

Golf Cart Rental Recommendations

You can easily get around the island via golf cart. There's golf cart parking underneath the house, in town, and at every restaurant and business around the island.
We recommend Getaway Cart Rentals, Island Cart Rentals, Hope Town Cart Rentals, Lighthouse Cart Rentals, and Elbow Cay Cart Rentals.

Boat Rental/Fishing Recommendations

There's a boat slip included with the house (at the Dorros Cove dock). For boat rentals, we recommend Island Marine, Barefoot Boat Rentals, and Blue Wave Rentals. If you're interested in fishing or boat tours, we'll be happy to put you in touch with a boat/fishing captain (deep sea, bonefishing, etc.). We have an extensive list of local contacts.

Food Shopping & Restaurant Recommendations

There are 3 grocery stores located on Elbow Cay - LVA, Vernon's Grocery, and Harbour View. However, the best place to shop is Maxwell's in Marsh Harbour (it has the largest variety). Be sure to check out these delicious restaurants located on Elbow Cay, as well: On Da Beach, Abaco Inn , Firefly, Mackey's , Willie's, Hope Town Inn and Marina, and Cap'n Jack's.
